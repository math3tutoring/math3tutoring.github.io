About

Math Cubed is an LA-Based Remote Private tutoring service. Anusha is the head tutor. She completed her Bachelor's in Electrical Engineering at Purdue University, and is currently a graduate student at UCLA in the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department.

With her previous experience of tutoring K-12 students and industry work experience, she found herself wanting to use previous knowledge to help others through tutoring and mentorships.

Math Cubed is dedicated to providing premium tutoring services, guaranteed to boost students' knowledge and interest in math.
